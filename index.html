Christian, Entrepreneur, Carpenter, Weightlifter, Coach 

Carpentry

I started working in high end residential construction as a sophomore in high school. Being home schooled really allowed me a tremendous amount of flexibility opening me up to a lot of opportunities. My junior year of high school I was working full time through the summers and part time through the school year, doing school 2 days a week and working the other 3 days. 

I have had the opportunity to be a part of the building community for 7 years now, working on all aspects of the project. Working through high school I was exposed to all aspects of the build, that is actually swinging a hammer. Then at the end of high school and more recently I have been able to transition out of doing the work and have gained experience being able manage and lead the job sites. With a wide variety of experiences in this field I feel adequately prepared to tackle and project that might arise.

Coaching

In junior high my dad built a gym in the basement of our house at the time, and I fell in love. Through high school I spent almost all of my free time down there in the basement or researching everything I could about anything to do with fitness and nutrition. I still am in love with the gym and now I get to take what I have learned and have been able to accomplish and share that with others. I started coaching people several years ago and have been able to train people helping some people lose 30-50lbs and help other hit big pr's.
